# 五种3D表示方法总览

## Layout
Five vertical cards arranged in a single row, each card representing one 3D representation method. A thin decorative line connects them at the bottom. Title "3D表示方法" at top center in large handwritten font.

## ZONES
- **Title Zone**: Top center, large text "3D表示方法" with a small 3D cube icon
- **Card 1 - Mesh**: Leftmost card, showing a triangulated surface
- **Card 2 - Voxel**: Second card, showing a 3D grid of small cubes
- **Card 3 - Point Cloud**: Center card, showing scattered dots on a surface
- **Card 4 - SDF**: Fourth card, showing concentric contour shells with a zero-level set highlighted
- **Card 5 - NeRF**: Rightmost card, showing a 5D coordinate function with rays
- **Bottom Zone**: Thin connecting line with small arrows showing implicit relationships
